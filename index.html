What Saransh is all about

Saransh's passion for developing impactful, innovative solutions is fueled by unifying the experiences of others with her diverse professional background in industries such as financial, judicial, and property management. With Saransh Co. acts as a catalyst for transformational change (inside and out of the workplace). We use our platform to hack the glass ceiling for women and minorities.

Coding Workshops

Learn the basics of programming for how it relates to you. Whether its for your personal business, your workplace, or for your child's birthday party these personalized coding workshops are created specifically for your experience level and with the goal in mind.

Speaking Engagements

For keyntotes of women and minorities in technology, or STEAM's influence and impact of education.

Teaching the World to Code

We're currently working on an open source project geared to remove the language barrier of code - both programatically and speaking. If you're passioante about education, we need your help building curriculum. If you know other languages, we need your help expanding to other spoken languages. If you love helping, we have a place for you. Come, join, and be a part of the community of people passionate about making a change so the entire world can learn how to code.
